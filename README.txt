
Recall Rumble

Developed By: Gladiatory Game (Parita Wattanasupt, Sammy Flemington, Lexi Cooke)

Usage Instruction:
Recall Rumble is an online competitive trivia app where users compete to get to the top of the leaderboard by completing quizzes in a variety of categories.
Points are awarded based on the speed and accuracy of the player’s answers. Users can create an account to track their progress and stats, 
follow other users to stay up to date on their friends’ (or enemies’) scores, and upload their own custom quizzes using the quiz creation feature to be 
played by other users.
